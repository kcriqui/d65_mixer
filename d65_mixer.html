Mixing is computed by summing CIE 1931 2° tristimulus values of each monochromatic line; the required blend is solved as non-negative optical powers that land the combined chromaticity on the D65 white point. The gamut is the convex hull of the primaries — coverage is the share of each standard gamut's area that falls inside it. Relative optical power is proportional to radiant watts per line.
