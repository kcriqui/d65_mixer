Mixing sums CIE 1931 2° tristimulus values of each monochromatic line; the blend is solved as non-negative powers landing the combined chromaticity on D65. The gamut is the convex hull of the primaries. In beam-corrected mode the required watts scale with each beam's spot area at the viewing distance (Ø = diameter + distance × divergence)², so a fatter or faster-diverging line needs more power for the same apparent brightness — this shifts which line is limiting but never the on-axis white point. Coherent Genesis MX / Taipan specs are from published datasheets; diode and DPSS beam figures are representative estimates. Beams are assumed co-aligned with spectrally neutral scattering.
